About Me

Hi, I'm Sachethan.V, a first-year undergraduate student pursuing a degree in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at the Global Academy of Technology. I'm passionate about solving problems with technology and exploring the potential of AI to make our lives easier.

This tool, the Number Series Predictor, combines my interest in mathematics and AI. It aims to predict the next three numbers in a sequence based on patterns identified from the given input.

I believe in blending traditional knowledge with modern technology to innovate and inspire. Through this project, I hope to showcase how AI can assist in understanding and extending mathematical concepts.
